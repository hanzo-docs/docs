# Comms

Summarize threads, recap meetings, and draft replies inside the tools your team already lives in.

## Slack

Mention `@Hanzo` in any channel or DM to ask a question, summarize a thread, or run an agent.

**Connect** — open the [console](https://console.hanzo.ai), pick **Slack**, and **Add to Slack** to authorize the workspace over OAuth. Invite `@Hanzo` to the channels you want it in.

## Zoom & Google Meet

Join a call to transcribe, summarize, and extract action items; the recap posts back to your workspace.

**Connect** — from the [console](https://console.hanzo.ai), connect **Zoom** and/or **Google Meet** over OAuth and authorize calendar access so Hanzo can join scheduled meetings.

## Microsoft Teams

Chat with Hanzo in Teams and summarize channels and meetings.

| Asset |
|-------|
| [`hanzo-ai-teams-v1.9.31.zip`](https://github.com/hanzoai/extension/releases/download/v1.9.31/hanzo-ai-teams-v1.9.31.zip) |

**Install** — in Teams: **Apps → Manage your apps → Upload an app → Upload a custom app** and select the ZIP (the Teams app package). Admins can publish it org-wide from the Teams admin center.

## Zendesk

Draft replies, summarize tickets, and suggest macros from the agent sidebar.

| Asset |
|-------|
| [`hanzo-ai-zendesk-v1.9.31.zip`](https://github.com/hanzoai/extension/releases/download/v1.9.31/hanzo-ai-zendesk-v1.9.31.zip) |

**Install (ZAF private app)** — in Zendesk Admin: **Apps and integrations → Apps → Zendesk Support apps → Upload private app** and select the ZIP.

Newest installers are always on the [latest release](https://github.com/hanzoai/extension/releases/latest).

> **One identity, everywhere.** Sign in with Hanzo ([IAM](/docs/iam) OAuth) or paste a `hk-` [API key](/docs/api-keys). The same credential works in every Hanzo surface — see [Integrations & Extensions](/docs/apps).
