# Docs & Office

Draft, summarize, rewrite, and analyze documents where they already live — Microsoft Office, Outlook, Google Workspace, and PDFs.

## Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)

A task-pane add-in that reads and edits the open document — draft copy, summarize a sheet, rewrite a slide.

| Asset | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| [`hanzo-ai-office-v1.9.31.zip`](https://github.com/hanzoai/extension/releases/download/v1.9.31/hanzo-ai-office-v1.9.31.zip) | Production manifest bundle |
| [`hanzo-ai-office-localhost-v1.9.31.zip`](https://github.com/hanzoai/extension/releases/download/v1.9.31/hanzo-ai-office-localhost-v1.9.31.zip) | Localhost build for development |

**Install (sideload the manifest)**

1. Download and unzip the Office asset; it contains a `manifest.xml`.
2. Put the manifest on a trusted catalog:
   - **Windows** — a shared folder set as a Trusted Add-in Catalog (**File → Options → Trust Center → Trusted Add-in Catalogs**).
   - **macOS** — copy it to `~/Library/Containers/com.microsoft.<app>/Data/Documents/wef`.
   - **Web** — **Insert → Add-ins → Upload My Add-in** and pick `manifest.xml`.
3. In Word, Excel, or PowerPoint: **Insert → Add-ins → My Add-ins → Hanzo**.

## Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, Slides, Gmail)

Draft, summarize, and edit across Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Gmail — no download; connect over OAuth.

**Connect** — open the [console](https://console.hanzo.ai), pick **Google Workspace**, and authorize with your Google account. Hanzo then appears in the Workspace side panel across Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Gmail.

## PDF

Summarize, query, and extract structured data from PDFs at [pdf.hanzo.ai](https://pdf.hanzo.ai) — sign in with Hanzo and drop in a file.

Newest installers are always on the [latest release](https://github.com/hanzoai/extension/releases/latest).

> **One identity, everywhere.** Sign in with Hanzo ([IAM](/docs/iam) OAuth) or paste a `hk-` [API key](/docs/api-keys). The same credential works in every Hanzo surface — see [Integrations & Extensions](/docs/apps).
