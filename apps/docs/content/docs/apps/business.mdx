# Business & CRM

Summarize records, draft content, and trigger actions against your CRM, contracts, docs, and store — connected over OAuth, no download required.

Every app here connects the same way: open the [console](https://console.hanzo.ai), pick the app, authorize with your account, and (where relevant) scope the objects Hanzo may read and write.

## Salesforce

Summarize accounts and opportunities, draft follow-ups, and update records from chat.

**Connect** — [console](https://console.hanzo.ai) → **Salesforce** → authorize the connected app; Hanzo respects your Salesforce object and field permissions.

## DocuSign

Summarize agreements, draft envelopes, and check signing status.

**Connect** — [console](https://console.hanzo.ai) → **DocuSign** → authorize over OAuth.

## Notion

Query, summarize, and draft into your Notion workspace.

**Connect** — [console](https://console.hanzo.ai) → **Notion** → authorize and pick the pages and databases to share.

## HubSpot

Summarize contacts and deals, draft emails, and update CRM records.

**Connect** — [console](https://console.hanzo.ai) → **HubSpot** → authorize over OAuth.

## Shopify

Answer store questions, summarize orders, and draft product copy.

**Connect** — [console](https://console.hanzo.ai) → **Shopify** → authorize the app for your store.

> **One identity, everywhere.** Sign in with Hanzo ([IAM](/docs/iam) OAuth) or paste a `hk-` [API key](/docs/api-keys). The same credential works in every Hanzo surface — see [Integrations & Extensions](/docs/apps).
