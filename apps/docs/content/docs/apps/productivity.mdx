# Productivity

Reach Hanzo from the tools you drive your day with.

## Clio

Summarize matters, draft correspondence, and query your practice data inside Clio (legal practice management).

**Connect** — open the [console](https://console.hanzo.ai), pick **Clio**, and authorize over OAuth; Hanzo respects your Clio permissions on matters, contacts, and documents.

## Raycast

Run Hanzo from the Raycast command bar — ask, draft, and run agents without leaving your keyboard.

**Install** — open Raycast, search the **Raycast Store** for **Hanzo**, and add the extension. Set your `hk-` key in the extension preferences, or run the **Sign in with Hanzo** command.

> **One identity, everywhere.** Sign in with Hanzo ([IAM](/docs/iam) OAuth) or paste a `hk-` [API key](/docs/api-keys). The same credential works in every Hanzo surface — see [Integrations & Extensions](/docs/apps).
