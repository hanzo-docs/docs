# Design tools

Bring Hanzo into your canvas — generate and rewrite copy, name and annotate layers, and run AI actions on a selection in Figma and Sketch.

## Figma

| Asset |
|-------|
| [`hanzo-ai-figma-v1.9.31.zip`](https://github.com/hanzoai/extension/releases/download/v1.9.31/hanzo-ai-figma-v1.9.31.zip) |

**Install**

1. Download and unzip the Figma asset (it contains `manifest.json`).
2. In the Figma desktop app: **Plugins → Development → Import plugin from manifest…**
3. Select the `manifest.json`. Run it from **Plugins → Development → Hanzo**.

## Sketch

| Asset |
|-------|
| [`hanzo-ai-sketch-v1.9.31.zip`](https://github.com/hanzoai/extension/releases/download/v1.9.31/hanzo-ai-sketch-v1.9.31.zip) |

**Install** — unzip and double-click the `.sketchplugin` bundle; Sketch installs it and adds **Hanzo** to the **Plugins** menu.

Newest builds are always on the [latest release](https://github.com/hanzoai/extension/releases/latest).

## Sign in

Open the plugin and **Sign in with Hanzo**, or paste a `hk-` key in its settings.

> **One identity, everywhere.** Sign in with Hanzo ([IAM](/docs/iam) OAuth) or paste a `hk-` [API key](/docs/api-keys). The same credential works in every Hanzo surface — see [Integrations & Extensions](/docs/apps).
