# Industry verticals

Purpose-built connectors for regulated and industry systems. Each connects over the platform's native app model, and sensitive records stay server-side — never in the browser.

## Epic (Healthcare)

Summarize a chart and draft clinical notes from within Epic.

- **What it does** — chart summary and note drafting for clinicians.
- **How it connects** — **SMART on FHIR**; launches inside Epic and reads via FHIR APIs.
- **Data handling** — PHI stays server-side and is never returned to the browser.

**Connect** — your Epic administrator registers the Hanzo SMART on FHIR app and grants the required scopes; clinicians then launch Hanzo from within Epic.

## Procore (Construction)

Summarize and draft RFIs on your Procore projects.

- **What it does** — summarize incoming RFIs and draft responses.
- **How it connects** — Procore OAuth, scoped to the projects you authorize.

**Connect** — [console](https://console.hanzo.ai) → **Procore** → authorize and select the company and projects.

## QuickBooks (Finance)

Summarize financials and answer questions about your books.

- **What it does** — summarize financial statements and reports.
- **How it connects** — QuickBooks Online OAuth.

**Connect** — [console](https://console.hanzo.ai) → **QuickBooks** → authorize the company file over OAuth.
