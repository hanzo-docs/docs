# Integrations & Extensions

Hanzo runs where you work. Install an extension, sideload an add-in, or connect an app over OAuth — and the same AI, tools, and agents follow you into your browser, your editor, your inbox, your CRM, and the desktop.

## One API key, one identity, everywhere

Every surface below is a thin client over the same foundation:

- **[`@hanzo/ai` gateway](/docs/gateway)** — one OpenAI-compatible base URL, `https://api.hanzo.ai/v1`, fronting every model and tool.
- **[`@hanzo/iam` identity](/docs/iam)** — one account across every app, org-scoped and SSO-ready.

You authenticate **once**, either way:

1. **Sign in with Hanzo** — OAuth through [Hanzo IAM](/docs/iam). Click **Sign in**, approve, done.
2. **Paste a `hk-` key** — create one in the [console](https://console.hanzo.ai) (see [API Keys](/docs/api-keys)) and paste it into the surface's settings.

The same key and the same identity work in every extension, add-in, and connected app. Switch orgs once and it applies everywhere.
