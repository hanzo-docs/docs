Hanzo Platform offers straightforward pricing with no hidden fees. Pay only for the resources you use.

## Pricing Model

All resources are billed **monthly**. You can scale up or down at any time, and charges are prorated to the hour.

| Category | Starting At | Details |
|----------|------------|---------|
| **Cloud VMs** | $5/mo | 6 tiers from Nano to Power Dedicated |
| **GPU Instances** | On request | NVIDIA T4, A100, H100 |
| **Block Storage** | $0.10/GB/mo | SSD and NVMe tiers |

## What Is Included

Every plan includes:

- Unlimited deployments
- Built-in monitoring and log streaming
- Automatic TLS certificates
- SSH and web terminal access
- Snapshot backups (daily)
- DDoS protection
- 99.9% uptime SLA

## No Egress Fees

Outbound data transfer is free on all plans. We do not charge for bandwidth.

## Free Tier

New accounts receive **$10 in credits** applied automatically. This is enough to run a Nano instance for two months or a Micro instance for one month.

<Callout type="info">
Credits expire after 60 days. Unused credits are not refundable.
</Callout>
