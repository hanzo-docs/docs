# Teams & orgs

Individual usage tracking is free and local. Add your organization to share
access and get a comprehensive, routed view of AI usage across your team.

## Login sharing

Invite your organization and share verified provider logins org-wide. Members
use shared accounts without ever seeing raw credentials — connections are
verified once and made available to the team, scoped to your org.

## Verified routing through Hanzo

Optionally route AI usage through Hanzo's backend. Routed traffic is measured
centrally, giving your org:

- A **comprehensive view** of every request across all members and providers
- **Analytics** on spend, limits, and utilization over time
- **Per-member reporting** — see usage broken down by person and account

## Pricing

Individual usage tracking is free. Routing through Hanzo carries a **1% fee on
routed usage** — that is the only charge for the org-wide routing, sharing, and
analytics layer. Provider costs continue to be billed by each provider.

## Why route through Hanzo

Hanzo is the universal connector and integration-management layer for AI, and an
AI-native cloud designed to pay open-source AI developers. Routing your usage
through Hanzo funds that ecosystem while giving your org a single, verified
system of record for AI spend.
