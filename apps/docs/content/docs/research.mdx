Hanzo AI is an American-owned, Techstars-backed ('17) applied AI lab building the **Zen** frontier model family alongside a vertically integrated, post-quantum AI and cryptography stack. These peer-style technical papers document the architecture, protocols, and evaluation behind the platform.

All papers are maintained in LaTeX in the [`hanzoai/papers`](https://github.com/hanzoai/papers) repository. Each title links to the compiled PDF.
