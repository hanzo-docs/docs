# Next Steps

Congratulations on getting started with Pars DAO! Here's what to do next to become an active participant.

## After Getting veASHA

### 1. Explore Active Resolutions

Visit [pars.vote/resolutions](https://pars.vote/resolutions) to see current proposals:

- **Active** - Currently in voting period
- **Pending** - In review period before voting
- **Queued** - Passed and awaiting execution
- **Executed** - Successfully completed

Start by reading and understanding proposals before voting.

### 2. Join a Committee

Find a Committee that matches your interests:

| Committee | Focus | Best For |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| Health (سلامت) | Humanitarian aid | Healthcare professionals, aid workers |
| Culture (فرهنگ) | Arts & heritage | Artists, historians, creators |
| Research (دانش) | Education | Academics, researchers |
| Infrastructure (سازندگی) | Technical systems | Developers, engineers |
| Consular (پیام) | Partnerships | Diplomats, community organizers |
| Security (امنیّت) | Safety | Security experts |
| Treasury (خزانه) | Finance | Financial professionals |
| Governance (داد) | Rules | Legal, governance experts |
| Venture (وقف) | Investments | Investment professionals |
| Impact (میزان) | Accountability | Auditors, analysts |

**To join:**
1. Stake minimum 1,000 veASHA
2. Submit application via governance forum
3. Committee reviews application
4. Vote or auto-admit if slots available
5. 30-day probation period

### 3. Participate in Discussions

Join the conversation:

- **Governance Forum** - [forum.pars.vote](https://forum.pars.vote)
- **Discord** - Real-time discussions
- **Town Halls** - Monthly community calls

### 4. Build Your Karma

Karma increases your voting power:

| Activity | Karma Earned |
|----------|--------------|
| Vote on Resolution | +5 |
| Submit approved proposal | +50 |
| Committee participation | +10/month |
| Grant completion | +25 |

Stay active to maintain Karma (1%/year decay when active, 10%/year when inactive).

## For Committee Members

### 1. Attend Meetings

- Weekly Committee syncs
- Monthly cross-Committee coordination
- Quarterly Town Halls

### 2. Review Proposals

Committee members should:

- Read all proposals in their domain
- Provide feedback during draft phase
- Vote on Committee-level decisions
- Help execute passed Resolutions

### 3. Contribute to Deliverables

Each Committee has required outputs:

- Reports (monthly/quarterly)
- Grant reviews
- Operational tasks
- Community engagement

### 4. Mentor New Members

Help onboard newcomers:

- Answer questions
- Share institutional knowledge
- Guide through first participation

## For Power Users

### 1. Delegate Management

If you hold significant veASHA:

- Accept delegations from others
- Communicate your voting positions
- Be responsive and accountable
- Consider running for Committee leadership

### 2. Submit Proposals

With 10K+ veASHA, you can submit Resolutions:

1. Draft in governance forum (7 days)
2. Incorporate community feedback
3. Formally submit
4. Campaign for votes
5. Execute if passed

### 3. Create Sub-DAOs

Launch initiatives under existing Committees:

- Identify unmet needs
- Build coalition of supporters
- Draft comprehensive proposal
- Secure Committee sponsorship

### 4. Guardian Consideration

During bootstrap phase, experienced members may be invited to Guardian Council:

- 7 multisig signers
- Emergency powers
- Sunset after 24 months
- High accountability

## FAQ

### How long should I lock my ASHA?

Longer locks = more voting power. Consider:
- 6 months: Trying out governance
- 12 months: Active participant
- 24+ months: Committed long-term

### Can I unlock early?

No, veASHA locks are binding. Plan your lock duration carefully.

### How do I increase my voting power?

1. Lock more ASHA
2. Extend lock duration
3. Build Karma through participation
4. Accept delegations

### What if I disagree with a passed Resolution?

- Participate in discussion before voting
- Vote against proposals you oppose
- Submit counter-proposals
- Work within the system to change direction

### How do I leave a Committee?

1. Announce intention in Committee
2. Complete handover of responsibilities
3. Committee removes your seat
4. Maintain veASHA if continuing in DAO
